Our Programmes

Samtse College of Education offers various programmes to prepare future educators and leaders. Below are some of the key programmes:

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)

This programme is designed to prepare competent teachers for primary and secondary schools.

Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE)

A one-year professional programme aimed at graduates who wish to pursue a teaching career.

Master of Education (M.Ed)

An advanced degree programme focusing on leadership, management, and specialized areas in education.
